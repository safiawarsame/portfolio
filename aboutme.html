About Me

Hi, my name is Safia Warsame. Im currently a student at Coder Academy.I'm going to graduate as a full stack developer in August,2021 with a diploma of information technology

I joined Coder Academy because I want to achieve my ambitions and build a career in the IT industry. Technology is slowly taking over the world and I want to be part of that.

My greatest passion is to learn new things and adapt to new environment where I’m being challenged and overcoming. The more I learn the more confident I am in creating the life I want. I’m passionate about increasing my wisdom because it allows me to get a clear perspective of my life.

As a black Muslim woman society doesn’t give us many opportunities because they look down on us. I want to prove that we can achieve all our goals and ambitions. Not many black female women are in the IT industry and I want to change that.
